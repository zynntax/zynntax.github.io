About The Game

The Banished Knight is a story-driven action experience where you play as a warrior wrongfully accused of treason and stripped of your title. Betrayed by those you once served, you are imprisoned and left to rot — until you manage a daring escape. Now free, you must fight through hostile lands, battle ruthless enemies, and uncover the truth behind the conspiracy that destroyed your honor. Along your journey, you will face difficult choices, powerful adversaries, and hidden secrets that test both your strength and your resolve.
